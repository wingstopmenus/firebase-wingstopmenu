⚠️ Wingstop Allergen Information

If you have food allergies, this section is important.

🧪 Major Allergens Present

Wingstop menu items may contain:

  • Wheat (breaded items)
  • Soy
  • Milk (butter sauces, ranch)
  • Eggs
  • Gluten
  • Celery (seasonings)

🍗 Items With Higher Allergen Risk

  • Boneless wings (breaded → wheat/gluten)
  • Ranch & Blue Cheese dips (milk, eggs)
  • Some sauces (butter-based)

✅ Safer Choices (Relatively)

  • Plain classic wings
  • Veggie sticks
  • Certain dry rub flavors

👉 However, cross-contamination is possible because food is prepared in shared kitchens. Always check the official allergen guide at your local store before ordering.
